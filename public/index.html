How to Use:

This application allows you to sort through generation I pokémon by type, weakness, height, and weight. Once you configure all the parameters you want, click the filter button in order to sort. Also, you can change the current song by pressing the change song button, as well as switch between light and dark mode. To bring up this menu again, just press the ? button on your keyboard!
